Multiple Variables Scatter Plot

Instructions

Use your mouse hover over the scatter plot to get information about payroll and amount of wins for US sports.

Using the legend on the right of the scatter plot click to add or remove major sports from the plot.

1. Look at one major sport, describe the relationship shown on the scatter plot.
hint: as payroll increases, what happens to amount of wins?.
2. Using the legend add two major sports. Provide 3 things you notice.
hint: how are they similar? How are they different?
3. Give an explanation for each thing you identified.
What do you notice? What does it make you wonder?
4. Does there appear to be an association between team salary and total team wins?
If so, describe the association, is it positive, negative, weak, or strong?
